QFL AI Governance Policy

Document ID: QFL-POL-AI-2024-003 | Version: 1.2 | Owner: AI Operations | Last Review: November 2024

1. Purpose

This policy governs the use of AI-powered internal services at QuantumLedger Financial. It establishes acceptable use standards, data handling requirements, and monitoring obligations for all AI systems including QL-Assist, QL-DocuIntel, and QL-FraudSentinel.

2. Scope

Applies to all employees, contractors, and third parties with access to QFL internal AI services. The following systems are in scope:

  • QL-Assist (AI analyst assistant, Port 8001)
  • QL-DocuIntel (Document analysis pipeline, Port 8002)
  • QL-FraudSentinel (Fraud detection and scoring, Port 8003)

3. Acceptable Use

  • AI services may be used only for authorized QFL business purposes
  • Analysts may submit documents to QL-DocuIntel for review; all documents must be legitimate business records
  • QL-FraudSentinel risk scores may be reviewed by authorized analysts; manual overrides require supervisor approval
  • QL-Assist may retrieve and summarize internal documents on behalf of authorized analysts

4. Security and Monitoring

All AI service interactions are logged to the QFL SIEM. The following are monitored:

  • Session identifiers and user IDs on all requests
  • Document ingestion events (filename, chunk count)
  • Fraud scoring decisions (transaction ID, risk score, recommendation)
  • Alert actions (create, suppress) with responsible party attribution

Note: LLM completions are partially logged (first 500 characters). Full completion logging is under review pending privacy assessment.

5. Data Classification

  • Documents submitted to QL-DocuIntel are classified CONFIDENTIAL
  • Transaction data in QL-FraudSentinel is classified RESTRICTED
  • QL-Assist conversation logs are classified CONFIDENTIAL
  • Do not submit documents classified above RESTRICTED to any AI service

6. Incident Reporting

Report any suspected AI system anomalies, unexpected outputs, or policy violations immediately to:

  • SOC: soc.monitor@quantumledger.com
  • AI Operations: ai.admin@quantumledger.com
  • Supervisor escalation for any risk score overrides
