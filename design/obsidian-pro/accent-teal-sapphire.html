Quick read

Both teal options pull toward green; both sapphire options stay firmly blue. Side-by-side, the question is: do you want the accent to feel cool (teal) or deep (sapphire)?

If you want maximum distance from the current indigo, steel teal #3a9b9b is the most different choice while still reading subtle. If you want subtlety with the safest seat at the table, sapphire #2c6fb8.
