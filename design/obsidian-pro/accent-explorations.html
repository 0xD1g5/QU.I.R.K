My recommendation

Your current #6675f7 is professional but generic. The decision is really about how brand-aligned you want the product to feel. Three viable paths:

① Keep indigo (current or lifted). Wins on safety, loses on distinction. Treat the 0xD1g5 mark as separate brand surface.
② Move to logo cyan #00e5cc. Best brand integration. Cyan + indigo + cyan-accent is the actual 0xD1g5 visual language. Watch the green proximity in the OK chip — solvable with a slightly cooler green.
③ Pick electric violet #9b6bff. Compromise: keeps the indigo family but pushes hard enough to feel intentional rather than default.

I'd reach for option ② (logo cyan) — the brand mark is too good to wall off. If you want to test it, say the word and I'll roll cyan through the full UI kit and re-render the preview cards so you can see the whole system together, not just isolated swatches.
