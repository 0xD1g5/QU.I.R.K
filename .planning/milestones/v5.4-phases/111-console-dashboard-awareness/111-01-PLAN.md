<note>
There are NO separate `/api/findings` or `/api/cbom` route handlers — findings and CBOM
components are returned as part of `ScanLatestResponse` from `GET /api/scan/latest`. The
REQUIREMENTS wording "`/api/findings` and `/api/cbom`" refers to the findings/CBOM *views*,
which are derived client-side from that single response. Therefore the `?segment=` param
attaches ONLY to `/api/scan/latest`; the per-segment filtering of findings/CBOM views happens
in the frontend (Plan 02) from the data that response carries. Do NOT invent new route handlers.
</note>
